Frequently Asked Questions

How do these sneakers fit? +
These sneakers run true to size. If you have wider feet, we recommend sizing up half a size. The velcro straps are adjustable for a customized fit.
Are they warm enough for winter? +
Yes! The plush sherpa/shearling lining provides excellent insulation and keeps your feet cozy in cold weather. Many customers wear them throughout winter with great comfort.
How do I care for the suede? +
We recommend applying a suede protector spray before first wear. Use a suede brush to remove surface dirt. Avoid getting them wet, and if they do get wet, let them air dry away from direct heat.
What's your return policy? +
We offer 30-day free returns with no questions asked. Simply request a return, and we'll send you a prepaid shipping label. Items must be unworn and in original condition.
How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 5-8 business days and is FREE. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $12.99. You'll receive tracking information within 24 hours of your order.
